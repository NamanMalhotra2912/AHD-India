Automatic Weather Station

Automatic Weather Station is a preconfigured, compact, robust and easy to use system provide faithful data in meteorological applications. It is designed for unattended monitoring remote sites which require high reliability, low power consumption and extended back-up battery capacity. The ease of implementation starts from the needs assessment through the standardized selection of options, and rapid delivery. The upgrading of the weather stations with additional sensors was designed for easy installation with a plug-and-play interface to allow for the immediate receiving of data.

Automatic Weather Station offer a select set of top-quality sensors to cover a wide array of meteorological measurement needs, following WMO guidelines in all measurements and calculations.

  • Easy and economical to install, maintain and upgrade.
  • Field-proven reliability and accuracy in harsh environments
  • Low power consumption for extended remote operations.
  • Plug-and-Play type of interface for selected sensors to get in use.
  • Measurements and calculations based on WMO guidelines.
  • Preconfigured Weather Station with three ready made reporting options.
  • Low Total-Life-Cycle Cost.

The AWS can be connected directly to TCP/IP networks over Ethernet, GSM and GPRS modems. The TCP/IP protocol stack is embedded into the data logger and no external serial vs. TCP/IP adapter modules are needed, meaning lower costs and better robustness.

AWS is consisting of Data logger enclosed in weather proof enclosure, Metrological sensor and mast to mount the sensors.

We provide the standard solution with parameters as :

  • Ambient Temperature
  • Ambient Relative humidity
  • Atmospheric pressure
  • Wind Speed
  • Wind Direction
  • Solar Radiation & rainfall

Apart from this we can customize the requirement depending on customer specifications.
